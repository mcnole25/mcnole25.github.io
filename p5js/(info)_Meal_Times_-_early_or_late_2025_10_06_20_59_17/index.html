There are 4 meals in a day: Breakfast, Lunch, Afternoon Meal, Dinner
Meal times guide:

- Eat breakfast within 1 hour of waking up
- Eat lunch about 4 hours after breakfast
- Eat afternoon meal equally in between lunch & dinner
- Eat dinner at least 3 hours before sleeping

How can I integrate more frequent, smaller meals in my day?
Only if your school or workplace allows it, bring things to eat with you.
