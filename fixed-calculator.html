Fixed Calculator

A basic Javascript calculator with fixed decimals (like 0.1 + 0.2) and added functions.
 Enable Short Thirds

Accepted functions:

+ > Addition
- > Subtraction
* > Multiplication
/ > Division
^ > Exponent
% > Modulo

( ) > Parentheses
pi > Pi
e > Euler's Number
sqrt > Square Root
cbrt > Cube Root
ln > Natural Log

sin > Sine (radians)
cos > Cosine (radians)
tan > Tangent (radians)
sindeg > Sine (degrees)
cosdeg > Cosine (degrees)
tandeg > Tangent (degrees)

floor > Round Down
round > Nearest Integer
ceil > Round Up
phi > Golden Ratio
abs > Distance To 0
sign > Negative/Positive

tau > Pi * 2
! > Factorial (up to 100)
