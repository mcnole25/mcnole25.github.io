Fixed Calculator

A basic Javascript calculator with fixed decimals (like 0.1 + 0.2) and added functions.
Note: This program is in early development and may have bugs.

Accepted functions:

+ > Addition
- > Subtraction
* > Multiplication
/ > Division
^ > Exponent
% > Modulo
( ) > Parentheses

pi > Pi
e > Euler's Number
sqrt > Square Root
cbrt > Cube Root
ln > Natural Log

sin > Sine (radians)
cos > Cosine (radians)
tan > Tangent (radians)
sindeg > Sine (degrees)
cosdeg > Cosine (degrees)
tandeg > Tangent (degrees)

floor > Round Down
round > Nearest Integer
ceil > Round Up
phi > Golden Ratio
