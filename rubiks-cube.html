Rubik's Sequence Calculator (3x3x3 only)

If you repeat a sequence of moves in the Rubik's cube, you'll eventually get back to where you started. How many repeats exactly? Powered by the cube.js library on Github.

 Sequence:
Clockwise face turn: U,D,F,B,L,R. Add a ' for an anticlockwise turn or a 2 for a 180 degree turn.
